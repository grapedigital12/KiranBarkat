About Me

The
Vision

01

I'm Sofia Reyes, a Los Angeles–based fashion designer with a passion for creating collections that blur the line between art and everyday wear. My design philosophy is rooted in cultural storytelling — drawing inspiration from the rich textures, colors, and rhythms of the city I call home.

With a background in garment construction and sustainable fashion practices, I design pieces that are as conscientious as they are beautiful — built to last, built to mean something.

Tierra SS26

A 12-piece ready-to-wear capsule collection inspired by the geological landscapes of the California desert. Each garment was draped, constructed, and dyed using natural pigments sourced locally — blending earthy terracotta tones with architectural silhouettes that echo desert erosion and canyon strata.
